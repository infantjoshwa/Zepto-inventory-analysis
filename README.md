🛒 Zepto Inventory Dashboard
📊 Project Overview
The Zepto Inventory Dashboard is an interactive data visualization project created using Power BI to analyze product inventory, revenue, pricing, and discount patterns.
The dashboard provides insights into:
Product revenue performance
Inventory distribution
Discount impact
Product pricing and stock levels
This dashboard helps understand which products generate the most revenue and how discounts affect sales performance.
🎯 Business Objectives
Monitor inventory and product availability
Identify top revenue generating products
Analyze discount patterns across products
Track average product pricing (MRP)
Understand product-wise revenue contribution

🔎 Key Insights
A few products contribute significantly higher revenue compared to others.
Average discount is around 15.46%, indicating moderate discount strategies.
Some products maintain high revenue despite lower quantity, suggesting strong demand.
Pricing distribution (MRP) impacts revenue trends across products.
